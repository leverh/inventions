Stone Age

The dawn of human innovation. Fire brought warmth and protection, stone tools enabled hunting and crafting, and early cave paintings marked humanity's first artistic expressions.

Renaissance

An explosion of knowledge and creativity. The printing press democratized information, telescopes revealed the cosmos, and detailed anatomical studies advanced medicine.

Modern Age

The age of electricity transformed daily life, the internet connected the world, and space exploration expanded our horizons beyond Earth's boundaries.

Future

Artificial intelligence augments human capability, space colonization becomes reality, and quantum computing solves previously impossible problems.
